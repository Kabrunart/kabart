Eugene Kabrun, Ukrainian Canadian Artist

The World In 3D

Since my childhood in Kyiv, Ukraine, I have been practising fine art, and my passion has only grown over the years. My art consists of a variety of genres and mediums – from landscapes, to still-lifes, to portraits. My diverse styles in painting allow me to capture and convey the beauty of sights that resonate deeply with both myself and my audience. I am located in Manitoba, Canada.

If you are interested in ordering a custom portrait, landscape or still-life painting, or wish to inquire about any paintings, or have any questions for me feel free to contact me at kabrunart@gmail.com.
